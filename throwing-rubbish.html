Throwing Rubbish

Impact Consequences Improvement


Impact

Throwing Rubbish in Local park has had a huge impact on the environment, health, community and economic. The environmental impacts are pollution of soil and water, harm to wildlife and damage to plants. The health impacts are spread of germs, unpleasant environment and injury risk. The community impacts are less community use, negative reputation and encourages littering. The economic impacts are higher cleaning costs, damage to facilities and reduced tourism and visitors.


Consequences

Throwing Rubbish in local park causes negative consequences for the environment and the community. Littering pollutes the soil, harms wildlife, and make the park look unclean and unpleasant. It also creates health risks by attracting pests and increase the chance of injuries due to sharp objects. Fewer people visit the park if the park is unclean and the community will have to spend more money to clean.
