Dried Plants

Impact Consequences Improvement


Impact

To impact of dried plants in the local park makes the park look unattractive and poorly maintained which reduces the beauty of the environment. Another one is loss of healthy green space. When plants dry out, it loses its greenery, shade and the fresh air. It also has a negative impact on wildlife which is being unable to provide food or protection for birds, insects and other small animals living in the park. Lastly, there is a huge fire risk which might cause a fire during hot seasons because dried plants can easily catch fire which is dangerous for the community.



Consequences

There are many consequences of dried plants in local park. Dried plants make the environment unattractive and lifeless facing with fewer visitor due to its poorly maintained environment. It also loss shades and comfort which make the park hotter and less comfortable. It reduces wildlife because it unable to provide food and protection. There will be poor air quality due to dried plants which cannot give its greenery, shade and fresh air. Lastly, there is a possibility that dried plants might catch fire during hot weather.



Improvement

To improve dried plants, it would be best the install regular water system, replace dried plants with new one, improving soil quality and regular maintenance. Installing sprinklers will ensure that the plants will get enough water.
